Training Regime

Well maybe you tought you will find something like starting strenght here, but you are wrong. I use a mix of kettlebells, clubbells and calistenics for my regime.

My routine:

I start Kettlebell Swings with 10x10 and every day increase the rep by one. After 20x10 i increase the weight. For the other kettebell exercises i start with 5x3 and increase the rep by one everyday until i reach 12x3 and then i increase the weight. For the heavy club exercises i do the different movements until i am tired. Wednesday is my recovery day so i do 20x3 burpees and pull ups 12x3 and handstand training.

Monday

  • Kettlebell Swings
  • Kettlebell turkish get ups
  • Heavy club shield cast
  • Heavy club inside circle
  • Heavy club inside pendulum
  • Heavy club pullover

Tuesday

  • Kettlebell front rack squat
  • Kettlebell clean

Friday
